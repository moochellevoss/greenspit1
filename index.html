Before you begin

Grypnôse is a mental training tool designed for climbers. It uses auto-hypnosis to support focus, preparation, and your relationship with your sport — nothing more, and nothing less.

Wanting to feel better is a worthy pursuit. At CAS, we believe wholeheartedly in every person's right to seek what brings them fulfilment. Grypnôse may bring you calm, confidence, and a deeper connection to your climbing. We hope it does.

It is not therapy. It does not have the capacity to heal, to diagnose, or to replace the work of a qualified professional. Using it in place of therapeutic support — or hoping it will serve that purpose — would be a mistake, and one we would not want for you.

If you or someone you know is experiencing psychological difficulty, please seek the support of a trained therapist. That is where healing lives.

Grypnôse is for your climbing. What happens beyond that is yours to navigate — and yours alone to take responsibility for.
